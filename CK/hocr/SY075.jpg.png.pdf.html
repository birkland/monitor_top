During the last part of the fourth quarter of 1941, an armature adjusting screw was added to the frame for the factory setting of pick»up current.

Caution: Never alter the setting of this arma- ture adjusting screw.

The MlA206 relay is different from other relays as it has a ﬁfth terminal which is connected to the frame of the relay by a wire. As long as the control contacts are closed, current is supplied to the start- ing winding through a capacitor in series with the winding. During the momentary starting period, a second capacitor is in parallel with the one that is in the circuit all the time.

UNSATISFACTORY REFRIGERATION

Unsatisfactory refrigeration complaints can be divided into two general classes: (1) Evaporator does not frost properly and (2) Evaporator frosts properly but the user is not satisﬁed with some part of the refrigeration service that the machine is supposed to render.

The machine is assumed to run all right: other— wise, the complaint would be “Machine Will Not Run.“

Many complaints of unsatisfactory refrigeration arise from a lack of understanding of variations in the frosting and in the performance of a machine clue to changes in the weather or load conditions, and of differences in frosting and performance between various models.

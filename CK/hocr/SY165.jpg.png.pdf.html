To replace the Monitor Top latch, remove the screws which hold it to the outer door panel. Then tip the latch toward the edge of the door on the latch side and pull the latch out of the door. The latch is replaced by reversing the procedure. To replace the escutcheon, slide it over the latch handle, hook the front edge over the latch base and push the escutcheon down so it snaps onto the latch base plate.

The latches used on the Flatop cabinets are, with a few exceptions, of the concealed type and generally of the same basic construction.

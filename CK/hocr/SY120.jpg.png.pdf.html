If the thermometer must be removed to read it, do so before the bulb is out of the liquid.

A thermometer can be hung in a refrigerator to get the air temperature. Before doing so, it should be ascertained that the door has not been opened much beforehand and that warm food has not been placed in the refrigerator. The thermometer should be placed so that its bulb is located in about the geometric center of the cabinet. The door should be opened only an instant while putting it in and should be left closed for at least ﬁfteen minutes. Then the reading should be made as quickly as possible after the door is opened.
